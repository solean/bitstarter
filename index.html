Thread wants to be a new, smarter way of storing, sharing, and syncing links. Imagine tapping a link in the Twitter app on your phone and immediately having that link in the cloud on Thread, synced across all of your devices. Of course, Thread will make it possible to sort and organize your links in whatever way you please, and integrated reminders will make sure you read that article later once you've finished your work. We also hope to make sharing links with friends, family, and coworkers as simple and elegant as possible.
